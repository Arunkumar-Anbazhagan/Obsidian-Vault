## What is Godot?
- Open-source game engine for 2D + 3D games (no licensing fees) [attached_file:1][web:2]
- Lightweight, high performance (vs heavy engines like Unity/Unreal)
- Node-based system: Everything is a "node" (intuitive hierarchy)
- GDScript: Beginner-friendly scripting (similar to Python indentation)
- Full ownership/control of your games/projects [attached_file:1]
