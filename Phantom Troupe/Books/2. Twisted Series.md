# THE TWISTED SERIES: CHARACTER ARCHITECTURE

## I. TWISTED LOVE (Alex & Ava)
* **Alex Volkov**: Cold billionaire, CEO, and tech genius. Driven by a quest for vengeance.
    * **Family**: Ivan Volkov (Uncle/Business Partner), Mikhail Volkov (Father - deceased), Katerina Volkova (Mother - deceased).
* **Ava Chen**: The "Sunshine" to Alex's "Grumpy."
    * **Family**: Michael Chen (Father/Antagonist), Wendy Chen (Mother - deceased), Josh Chen (Older Brother).

## II. TWISTED GAMES (Rhys & Bridget)
* **Rhys Larsen**: Elite bodyguard; former Navy SEAL. Works for Christian Harper’s security firm.
* **Bridget von Ascheberg**: Princess (later Queen) of Eldorra.
    * **Family**: King Edvard (Grandfather), Prince Andreas (Brother - abdicated), Andreas von Ascheberg (Cousin/Rival).
    * **Allies**: Mikaela Brahe (Best friend in Eldorra).

## III. TWISTED HATE (Josh & Jules)
* **Josh Chen**: Ava’s brother; a charming but arrogant ER Doctor.
    * **Connection**: Alex Volkov's former best friend (reconciled later).
* **Jules Ambrose**: High-achieving law student with a secret criminal past.
    * **Family**: Adeline Miller (Vain Mother), Alastair (Predatory Stepfather).
    * **Enemies**: Max (Manipulative ex-boyfriend/accomplice).

## IV. TWISTED LIES (Christian & Stella)
* **Christian Harper**: CEO of Harper Security; a "moral-less" manipulator and Stella’s landlord.
    * **Connections**: Part of the "Valhalla Club" with Alex Volkov.
* **Stella Alonso**: Introverted influencer.
    * **Family**: The Alonso Family (High expectations, unsupportive of her fashion dreams).
    * **Threat**: Julian (Stalker from her past).

---

## III. THE NEXT GENERATION (End of Series Epilogue)
* **The Volkovs**: Alex and Ava have twins (Niko and Sofia).
* **The von Aschebergs**: Rhys and Bridget have a daughter, Camille.
* **The Chens**: Josh and Jules are engaged (later married in the 'Kings of Sin' cameos).
* **The Harpers**: Christian and Stella are together, and Stella launches her own brand.
